Use this service to join the register of apprenticeship training providers so that you can train apprentices in the UK.

You do not have to complete your application in one go. You can save your details and finish it later.

You’ll need to allow at least 12 weeks for Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) to process your application.

Start now

Before you start

To join the register of apprenticeship training providers, your organisation will need to have:

  • a UK provider reference number (opens in a new window or tab)
  • an Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) registration number (opens in a new window or tab)
  • a UK business address
  • an individual or team that has experience in developing and delivering training

If your organisation is applying to the main or employer provider route, it must have been trading for at least 12 months.

If your organisation is applying to the supporting provider route, it must have been trading for at least 3 months.

If your organisation has had an Ofsted inspection, it must have been in further education and skills for apprenticeships. Your organisation must not have been graded ‘inadequate’ in the last 3 years.

If your organisation does not have a grade for apprenticeships, we will accept one for overall effectiveness.
