Introduction and what you'll need

Delivering apprenticeship training

For this section, you’ll need to know:

  • who has overall accountability for apprenticeships
  • your organisation's management hierarchy for apprenticeships
  • your management hierarchy’s expectations for quality and high standards in apprenticeship training
  • who’s responsible for quality and high standards in apprenticeship training
  • who’s responsible for developing and delivering training
  • the sectors your organisation will offer apprenticeships in
  • the experience and qualifications of the people who will deliver training

You’ll also need to upload your organisation’s policy for professional development of employees. We’ll ask for examples of how this policy is implemented and how it’s reflected in teaching and training.

It must be specific to your organisation and signed by a senior employee. For example, a director or CEO.
