Introduction and what you'll need

Planning apprenticeship training

For this section, you’ll need to know:

  • what apprenticeship training your organisation will offer
  • how your organisation will engage with end-point assessment organisations (EPAO’s) or awarding bodies
  • who we can contact about those apprenticeships
  • how your organisation will support apprentices
  • how many starts your organisation forecasts for the next 12 months
  • if your organisation is ready to deliver against these forecasts
  • if your organisation will recruit new staff against these forecasts
  • the typical ratio of staff and learners
  • how your organisation will deliver off the job training
  • the main address where apprentices will be trained
