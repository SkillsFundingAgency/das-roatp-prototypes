What happens next

There are 3 RoATP assessment teams that will assess your application. They are:

  • the gateway team - they assess your organisation, the people involved in your organisation and criminal and compliance checks
  • the financial health assessment team - they assess your organisation’s financial evidence
  • the assessor team - they assess the remaining application answers, policies and processes

You’ll need to allow at least 12 weeks for them to process your application.

If your answers are unclear, we may ask for clarification. You must reply within 5 working days or we may fail your application.

All communication will go to the email address you used to sign in and make this application.

If you have any questions, want to make a change or withdraw your application, email helpdesk@manage-apprenticeships.service.gov.uk or call 08000 150 600.

For more information, you can read our help articles for training providers (opens in a new window or tab).

As a new apprenticeship training provider, your organisation may be inspected by Ofsted within the first 3 years of receiving funding from ESFA.

To prepare for this, you should evaluate your organisation against:

  • the Education Inspection Framework (opens in a new window or tab)
  • the Further Education and Skills Inspection Handbook (opens in a new window or tab)
