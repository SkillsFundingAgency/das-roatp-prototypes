Financial health assessment

This section is to ensure your organisation’s financial performance and ability to meet ongoing financial commitments meet ESFA’s expectations.

You may need to provide your organisation’s:

  • financial figures
  • full financial statements or management accounts

Based on your answers and uploads, your organisation will receive a financial health grade.
