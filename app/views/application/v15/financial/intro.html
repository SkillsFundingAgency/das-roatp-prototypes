Introduction and what you'll need

Financial health assessment

This section is to ensure your organisation’s financial performance and ability to meet ongoing financial commitments meet ESFA’s expectations.

In this section we’ll ask:

  • if your organisation has a UK ultimate parent company
  • details of what your organisation is

Based on your answers you may need to:

  • provide your organisation’s financial figures
  • upload your organisation’s full financial statements or management accounts
  • upload your UK ultimate parent company's consolidated financial statements or financial statements for its subsidiary companies (if your organisation has one)
