Criminal and compliance checks

This section is to ensure public funds, employers and apprentices are protected.

Checks on your organisation

We’ll ask if your organisation has things like composition with creditors or any safeguarding or whistleblowing issues.

If it has, you’ll need to provide a brief summary including relevant dates.

Checks on the people who are in control of your organisation

We’ll ask if anyone who’s in control of your organisation has had things like criminal convictions or been investigated for fraud or irregularities.

If anyone has, you’ll need to provide a brief summary including relevant names and dates.

Based on your answers, we may contact you for further information.
