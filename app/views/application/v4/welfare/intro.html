Apprenticeship welfare

In this section we’ll ask you to upload your organisation’s:

  • continuity plan
  • equality and diversity policy
  • safeguarding policy
  • prevent duty policy (if needed)
  • health and safety policy

All policies must be specific to your organisation, apprentices and trainers. They must also be stamped and signed by a senior employee. For example, a director or CEO.

We will not accept any uploads that are generic or taken from a third party.
