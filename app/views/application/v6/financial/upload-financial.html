Your organisation’s financial evidence

Upload your organisation’s financial statements covering the last 12 months

Financial statements must include:

  • a ‘balance sheet’, showing the value of everything the organisation owns, owes and is owed on the last day of the financial year
  • a ‘profit and loss account’, showing the organisation’s sales, running costs and the profit and loss it made over the financial year
  • notes about the accounts
  • a breakdown of creditors and borrowings

The financial statements you upload should be signed and filed with Companies House.
