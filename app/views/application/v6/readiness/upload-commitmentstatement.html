Commitment statement template

Upload your organisation’s commitment statement template

Your organisation can use ESFA’s commitment statement, take one from a third party or have its own.

It must include:

  • the planned content and schedule for training
  • what is expected and offered by the employer, the training organisation and the apprentice

The commitment statement must also be signed by the apprentice and training organisation.

Find out more about the commitment statement in the apprenticeship funding rules (opens in a new window).
