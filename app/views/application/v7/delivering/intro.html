Introduction and what you'll need

Delivering apprenticeship training

For this section, you’ll need to know:

  • who’s accountable for apprenticeships
  • your organisation's management hierarchy
  • what sectors your organisation will offer apprenticeships in
  • which employees have experience in those sectors
  • how employees experience in those sectors is maintained and kept relevant through professional development
  • how employees experience in teaching and training is maintained and kept relevant through professional development

You’ll also need to upload your organisation’s policy for professional development of employees.

It be specific to your organisation and signed by a senior employee. For example, a director or CEO.
