Introduction and what you'll need

Delivering apprenticeship training

For this section, you’ll need to know:

  • your organisation's management hierarchy for apprenticeships
  • your management hierarchy’s high standards in apprenticeship training
  • who’s responsible for developing and delivering training
  • who has overall accountability for apprenticeships
  • the sectors your organisation will offer apprenticeships in
  • who has experience in those sectors
  • how employees experience in those sectors, teaching and training is maintained through professional development

You’ll also need to upload your organisation’s policy for professional development of employees.

It must be specific to your organisation and signed by a senior employee. For example, a director or CEO.
