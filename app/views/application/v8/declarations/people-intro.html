Introduction and what you'll need

Criminal and compliance checks on who’s in control of your organisation

This section is to ensure public funds, employers and apprentices are protected.

You’ll need to provide a brief summary if anyone who’s in control of your organisation has:

  • had any criminal convictions
  • failed to pay back funds to ESFA or any other public body
  • been investigated for fraud or irregularities
  • had a contract terminated by a public body
  • had early withdrawal from a contract with a public body
  • breached tax payments or social security contributions
  • been removed from any charity register
  • been removed from the Register of Removed Trustees
  • been made bankrupt
  • been subject to insolvency or winding up proceedings

Depending on the answers you give, we may ask for further information.
