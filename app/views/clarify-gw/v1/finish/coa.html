Terms and conditions

Do you agree to the terms and conditions of submitting an application?

These terms and conditions are to ensure government funding and apprentices are protected.

By submitting an application you agree to:

  • all application answers being accurate and specific to your organisation
  • ESFA using information in the public domain to support the assessment of application answers
  • tell ESFA if any application answers change
  • tell ESFA if the ownership of your organisation changes
  • cooperate within 5 working days if ESFA makes an enquiry about your organisation or this application

By submitting an application, you understand that:

  • incorrect application answers could lead to civil or criminal proceedings
  • your application may be rejected if there’s evidence of collusion with other apprenticeship training providers or organisations
  • your application may be rejected if your organisation falls under the ‘funding higher risk organisations and subcontractors’ (or any policy that replaces it)

    • {% endif %}

ESFA may ask for further information on any application answers - they may withdraw your application if you do not comply.

ESFA reserve the right to change these terms and conditions at any time. Your organisation will automatically be obligated to meet the changes made to these terms and conditions. You’ll be able to view these changes on the RoATP GOV.UK page (opens in a new window or tab).
