Start now

Before you start, all providers must have a:

  • UK provider reference number
  • Information Commissioner's Office registration number
  • UK business address
  • minimum Ofsted grade of 3 for apprenticeship or overall effectiveness under Further Education and Skills in the last 3 years

Main and employer providers must be:

  • actively trading for at least 12 months
  • ready to train apprentices within the first 12 months of joining the register

Supporting providers must be actively trading for at least 3 months.
