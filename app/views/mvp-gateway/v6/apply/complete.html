What happens next

We have sent your application to the following RoATP application assessment teams:

  • gateway team - they assess your organisation, the people involved in your organisation and criminal and compliance checks
  • financial health assessment team - they assess your organisation’s financial evidence
  • assessor team - they assess the remaining application answers, policies and processes

If your application answers are unclear, we may ask for further information. You must reply within 5 working days or we may withdraw your application.

You will need to allow at least 12 weeks for us to process your application. It may take longer because this is a private beta service. We will send you an email when there’s an update with your application.

Once your application has been assessed, your organisation will receive an outcome, a financial health grade and next steps.

Find out more about how your organisation is assessed for financial health (opens in a new window or tab).

If you want to know more about how the teams currently assess applications, how outcomes and financial health grades are given and next steps you can read ‘section 7 what happens next’ (opens in a new window or tab). Some processes may differ, as the guidance is based on the current Bravo service.

If you have any questions, want to make a change or withdraw your application, email roatp.support@education.gov.uk.
