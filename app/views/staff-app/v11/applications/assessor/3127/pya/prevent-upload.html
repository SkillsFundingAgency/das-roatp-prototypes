Application question Upload your organisation’s Prevent duty policy

Check if the upload includes how the organisation will:

  • promote the policy
  • get commitment to the policy
  • train employees in implementing the policy
  • have a legal responsibility to fulfil the Prevent duty statement
  • protect apprentices and employees from radicalising influences
  • ensure apprentices and employees are resilient to extreme narratives
  • identify changes in behaviour of apprentices and employees
  • deal with any issues raised by apprentices or employees

What is the outcome of this check?

Pass this check if the upload:

  • includes all of the above bullet points
  • is specific to the organisation, apprentices and trainers
  • is signed by a senior employee

Fail this check if the upload:

  • does not include all of the above bullet points
  • is not specific to the organisation, apprentices and trainers
  • is not signed by a senior employee
